# Product Overview

**numu** is a growth-focused habit tracking mobile application built with Flutter.

## Core Features

- **Habit Tracking**: Binary (done/not done) and value-based (quantitative) habit tracking
- **Categories**: Organize habits into custom categories with icons and colors
- **Reminders**: Schedule notifications and alarms for habits with flexible scheduling
- **Statistics**: Track streaks, period progress, and completion rates
- **Customization**: Multiple theme options, configurable week start day
- **Home Dashboard**: Daily overview of habits and tasks with progress tracking

## Key Concepts

- **Binary Habits**: Simple completion tracking (e.g., "Did you meditate today?")
- **Value Habits**: Quantitative tracking with units and targets (e.g., "Drink 8 glasses of water")
- **Streaks**: Current and longest streaks for habit consistency
- **Period Progress**: Track completion rates over daily, weekly, or custom periods
- **Active Days**: Configure which days a habit is active (all days, weekdays, weekends, or custom)
- **Quality Layer**: Optional quality tracking for habits (e.g., "How well did you do?")
- **Time Windows**: Optional time constraints for when habits should be completed

## User Experience

The app emphasizes simplicity and growth mindset, helping users build sustainable habits through clear visualization, flexible tracking options, and motivational feedback.
